1. Download an app from Google Play Store called "Hosts Go"

2. Open the app and press HOSTS EDITOR

3. Make it to look like this:

141.95.12.116 www.growtopia1.com

141.95.12.116 www.growtopia2.com

141.95.12.116 growtopia1.com

141.95.12.116 growtopia2.com

4. Go back to the start menu in "Hosts Go" and press START and Open Growtopia and play
